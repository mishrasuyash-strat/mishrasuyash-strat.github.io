About

I'm a senior AI product leader and independent researcher based in Zürich. Day to day I turn hard, ambiguous problems into decision systems that hold up under scrutiny; on my own time I formalise the ideas underneath — from causal decision units to multi-agent coordination and sheaf-theoretic ways of gluing local judgments into one defensible whole.
