For a while my writing has lived in private notes, half-finished decks, and
papers that never quite made it out the door. This site is an attempt to fix
that: one place for the essays and the research, published as I go.

## What I'll write about

Mostly the things I actually spend my time on:

- **Decision-making under uncertainty** — not the textbook version, but the messy
  kind where the stakes are real and the answer has to survive an audit.
- **Multi-agent systems** — how you get many specialised models or agents to
  agree on something you'd be willing to sign your name to.
- **The mathematics underneath** — causal inference, game theory, and a fair bit
  of category and sheaf theory, kept as concrete as I can make it.

I'll try to write the version I wish I'd been able to read: precise about what's
new, honest about what isn't, and light on hype.

> The goal isn't to sound clever. It's to make hard reasoning legible — to
> myself first, and then to anyone who has to trust it.

## How posts work here

Every post is a Markdown file. Code renders with syntax highlighting, and posts
that need mathematics can turn it on. The next post shows both.
